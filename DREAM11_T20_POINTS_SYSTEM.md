# Dream11 Fantasy Cricket T20 Points System

Complete scoring rules for T20 fantasy cricket on Dream11.

---

## Batting Points

| Rule | Points |
|------|--------|
| Run | +1 |
| Boundary Bonus | +4 |
| Six Bonus | +6 |
| 25 Run Bonus | +4 |
| Half-Century Bonus (50 runs) | +8 |
| 75 Run Bonus | +12 |
| Century Bonus (100 runs) | +16 |
| Dismissal for a duck (Batter, Wicket-Keeper, & All-Rounder) | -2 |

### Important Batting Notes
- Any player scoring a century will only get points for the century. No points such as 30 run Bonus or Half-century Bonus will be awarded together.
- If any runs are scored on an overthrow, points for those runs will be credited to the batter on strike for that ball.
- If the overthrow goes for a boundary, the batter will not receive extra Boundary Bonus points.

## Fielding Points

| Rule | Points |
|------|--------|
| Catch | +8 |
| 3 Catch Bonus | +4 |
| Stumping | +12 |
| Run out (Direct hit) | +12 |
| Run out (Not a direct hit) | +6 |

### Important Fielding Notes
- A direct hit is inflicted by the fielder who is the only one to touch the ball after the batter faces the delivery.
- In all other cases, points will be awarded only to the last 2 fielders who touch the ball.
- Players taking more than 3 catches will also get 4 points as 3 Catch Bonus. For example, if a player takes 6 catches, they will get 4 points as bonus (not 8 points).

## Economy Rate Points
**Minimum requirement: Bowler must have bowled at least 2 overs**

| Economy Rate (Runs Per Over) | Points |
|-----|--------|
| Below 5 runs per over | +6 |
| Between 5-5.99 runs per over | +4 |
| Between 6-7 runs per over | +2 |
| Between 10-11 runs per over | -2 |
| Between 11.01-12 runs per over | -4 |
| Above 12 runs per over | -6 |

---

## Strike Rate Points
**Minimum requirement: 10 balls must be played (Does NOT apply to bowlers)**

| Strike Rate (Runs Per 100 Balls) | Points |
|-----|--------|
| Above 170 runs per 100 balls | +6 |
| Between 150.01-170 runs per 100 balls | +4 |
| Between 130-150 runs per 100 balls | +2 |
| Between 60-70 runs per 100 balls | -2 |
| Between 50-59.99 runs per 100 balls | -4 |
| Below 50 runs per 100 balls | -6 |

### Important Strike Rate Notes
- Negative points for low batting Strike Rates are only applicable for individual Strike Rates of 70 runs per 100 balls or below.
- Strike rate points do NOT apply to bowlers.

## Playing Substitute

| Type | Points |
|------|--------|
| Concussion, X-Factor, or Impact Player | +4 |

### Substitute Rules
- All X-Factor substitutes will get 4 points for being announced and score points for any contributions they make as per the Fantasy Point System.
- Concussion & COVID-19 Substitutes will be awarded 4 points for making an appearance (only for T20s, ODs, Tests, T10s and The Hundred) and will be awarded points for all contributions they make as per the Fantasy Point System.
- Substitutes other than Concussion, COVID-19, or X-Factor substitutes will NOT be awarded points for any contribution they make.
- If a player gets replaced by an X-Factor substitute but later comes back on the field, they will get points for their contributions. However, if a player who was not part of the announced lineups comes to the field as a substitute (except Concussion and COVID-19 substitutes), they will NOT get points.

## General Rules & Important Points

### Player Eligibility & Substitutes
- A player who has been transferred from one team to another might still be available for selection for their older team until the next scheduled team update. However, no points will be attributed to the player in such a situation.
- If a player is announced in the starting eleven but later is unable to start the match, they will NOT score any points.
- The player who plays as the replacement will earn points for the match (including the 4-point lineup bonus).

### Match & Points Adjustments
- **No points will be awarded for any actions during a Super Over or a Super Five.**
- No further adjustments will be made to the points awarded once a match has been marked as completed and winners have been declared.
- Points awarded for a LIVE game will be subject to change only when the match status is "In Progress" or "In Review".
- Final Fantasy Cricket points can be viewed after the match is over, when the match status shows as 'Completed'.

### Specific Format Notes
- More than 11 players from either team can play in Other OD/Other T20/Other Test matches.
- No points will be awarded for Strike, Economy, or Maidens in 'The Hundred'.

## Quick Reference: Bonus Points

### Batting Bonuses
- **Half-Century**: 50+ runs = +8 points
- **3 Runs Bonus**: Every 25 runs after first = +4 points each (at 25, 50, 75 etc.)
- **Century**: 100+ runs = +16 points (replaces all other batting bonuses)

### Bowling Bonuses
- **Maiden Over**: No runs in a complete over = +12 points
- **3 Wicket Bonus**: 3 wickets = +4 points
- **4 Wicket Bonus**: 4 wickets = +8 points
- **5 Wicket Bonus**: 5 wickets = +12 points
- **LBW/Bowled Bonus**: +8 points per LBW or Bowled dismissal

### Fielding Bonuses
- **3 Catch Bonus**: 3+ catches = +4 points

---

## Points Update Timeline
- Fantasy Cricket points keep changing as the actual match progresses.
- Points might change while the match status is "In progress" or "Waiting for review".
- Final Fantasy Cricket points can be viewed after the match is over, when the match status shows as 'Completed'.
